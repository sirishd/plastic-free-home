A reasonable note: You can't eliminate plastic entirely, and trying to do it all at once is the fastest way to give up. The "high impact" cards are where your effort pays off most — food contact and anything you sleep on, scrub with, or inhale around. Start there, then work outward. Thrift and second-hand are often the most sustainable options of all, even if the item is synthetic.
